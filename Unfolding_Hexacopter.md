**Steps**

- Loosen the plate and the pivot screws (yellow and orange) for each of the pivoting arms until the nut is at the end of the screw’s threading
- Pivoting are the 4 arms facing forward and backward (all of the arms EXCEPT for the one facing left and right, which are perpendicular to the landing gear legs)
- Since 2 screws are being loosened for 4 arms, a total of 8 screws will be loosened.
- Rotate the arms to line up with the inner arm screw holes and avionics cover holes.
- Put an inner arm screw through this entire group and tighten with pliers and a screwdriver. 
- The screw should go through: the 3D print avionics cover hole, top plate, top hole of the arm, bottom hole of the arm, bottom plate, and secured with a nut.
- There is 1 for each of the 4 arms, so a total of 4 screws will be added.
- Tighten the plate and pivot screws in the same way. 
- Connect the  4 ESC’s to their respective arm (1 red, 1 blue, and 1 black wire for each)
- There is a yellow flag on every other ESC wire and its corresponding arm wire to prevent confusion with nearby ESC’s that may have moved over - be sure you connect yellow flag wires together and non-yellow flag wires together
- Make sure to check for wires out of the way of screws
